How It Works

Our technology integrates directly with marketplace backends such as Amazon Seller Central, Shopify, Walmart, and others. Each AI-Agent securely extracts and learns from historical and real-time data aligned to its area of responsibility. For example, the Marketing AI-Agent gathers all relevant advertising and sales performance data to autonomously implement and scale growth strategies.

Aurora9 offers two modes of operation to meet different business needs:

  • Autonomous Mode – AI agents independently strategize and execute without requiring human approval.
  • Progressive Mode – AI agents suggest data-backed decisions and wait for user approval before executing actions.

Our platform is powered by the robust and secure IBM Watsonx Cloud, ensuring enterprise-grade performance, AI governance, and scalability. With this foundation, Aurora9 delivers a governance-first, action-oriented, and explainable AI system designed to meet the trust and compliance requirements of today’s ecommerce leaders.
